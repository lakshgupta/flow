# Flow User Manual

Flow is a **local-first Markdown workspace** for capturing notes, tracking tasks, and running commands — all stored as plain files on your machine.

## What you can do with Flow

- **Take notes** in a structured, linkable graph of Markdown documents.
    
- **Track tasks** with statuses like Ready, Running, Done, Success, Failed, or Interrupted.
    
- **Run commands** defined inside your workspace from the CLI or GUI.
    
- **Visualize connections** on an interactive graph canvas that shows how your work fits together.
    
- **Search everything** — titles, descriptions, tags, and content — across your whole workspace.
    
- **Work locally or globally** — tie a workspace to a project directory, or keep a personal workspace that follows you everywhere.
